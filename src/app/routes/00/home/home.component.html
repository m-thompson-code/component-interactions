Reactive Parent to Child Component Communication

There is more to Component interaction than direct parent to child Component communication. However, it is common to produce features in a top-down archecture when working with Angular since its structure is starts from a root and branches out to children dependencies.

There are many options to consider before committing to a bi-directional data-flow which is why it's commonly questioned as a code smell once introduced into the codebase.
